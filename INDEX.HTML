LIBRARY DEPARTMENT

The library department is responsible for managing and maintaining the library resources, including books, journals, digital media, and other materials. The department ensures that the library is organized, accessible, and provides a conducive environment for learning and research. It also offers various services such as book lending, reference assistance, and information literacy programs to support the academic community. The library department plays a crucial role in fostering a culture of reading and knowledge sharing among students, faculty, and staff.
